Hello! My name is Steven Diep and I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at Portland State University.

I have previously worked at CAL Charter as an intern for their Summer Coding Camp and currently work as a student grader for the computer sciences classes at Portland State University. During my time working in these positions, I got to apply my knowledge of computer science and programming and helped other students succeed in their studies. This summer, I will be working as a software engineering intern.

In addition to my work experience, I have created many projects in my free time and for my courses. One of which is a Discord bot that can host hangman games across many different servers. Through my coursework and projects, I have gained experience working with C/C++, Python, Java, and SQL.

After my graduation next year, I hope to become a software engineer.
